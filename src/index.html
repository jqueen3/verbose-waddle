Style Guide 1.0

Purpose/Theme

The purpose is to display CSS skills through the medium of CSS Zen Garden. The theme we selected is outer space. Ideas we have to best portray this theme are plentiful. The page opens to show a moon and its light cast over the darkness of space, as stars drift lazily behind the elements on the screen. Each section will be portrayed by a planet with the title having its own planet and revolving moon. Some of the links will be portrayed in the shape of a constellation. The very bottom of the screen will have outline, shape, and coloring of the earth with the remaining elements over top.

HTML Elements

Two main components that will run as keyframe animations will be a starfield and a screen passover of different space pop culture references. These will include Darth Vader's Tie fighter, a space invader, the Starship Enterprise, Nyan Cat, and Others. The mini references will pass over the screen at random intervals, and will be spaced out as to not overcrowd the screen with elements. The starfields will run behind the elements in a slow rotating pattern so as to give the apearance of floating through space.
